Overview

We offer a benchmark suite together with an evaluation server, such that authors can upload their results and get a ranking. We offer a dataset that contains more than 25,000 pictures, including 15,403 images for training set, 5,000 images for validation set and 5,000 images for testing set. If you would like to submit your results, please follow the instructions on our submission page.

Note: We only display results with relatively detailed descriptions.

Contributors Description
MJDG (Multi-person Joint Detection and Grouping): Sheng Jin (Tsinghua University), Wentao Liu(Tsinghua University) Our method is a pure bottom-up pose estimation method based on “Associative Embedding: End-to-End Learning for Joint Detection and Grouping”. We use 4 stage stack-hourglass network to extract both high-level and low-level features, and predict 1D “tag” to identify its group assignment. The tag is trained to distinguish different people, using pull&push losses. We perform bottom-up pose detection and greedy grouping to obtain the final results. Multi-scale testing (2, 1.5, 1.25, 1, 0.75, 0.5) is used. Unlike previous works, no single person pose estimation model is used to refine the result. Only train-val data from MHP Dataset is used.
